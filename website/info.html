history
forming in early 2017, honeymoon has always had a goal to create not just memorable songs, but a visual + auditory experience not easily forgotten. they recorded the 14:43 EP in may 2017 + played their first show august 1 2017. they quickly got back to writing and recording, tracking both “perfect” and “like suffering” in november 2017 + releasing them in may 2018. they toured the midwest in summer 2018 + got to writing + recording a full-length record as soon as they got back. the record was tracked, engineered and produced by pete milkovich at bad racket recording studios in cleveland ohio in january 2019.
